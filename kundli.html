Kundali and dosh FAQ

What if I do not know exact birth time?

Share the closest known time and mention that it is approximate. The Acharya can advise what level of review is possible and whether birth-time correction is needed.

Can I book only for Kaal Sarp, Mangal or Pitra Dosh?

Yes. Dosh-specific review is available, but the chart should be checked before selecting any pooja, hawan or gemstone remedy.

Will gemstone be suggested in every Kundali consultation?

No. Gemstone guidance is shared only when suitability supports it. The page does not promise a forced stone sale.
