We keep information only as long as needed for service delivery, support, tax, accounting, dispute handling and legal compliance. You can request deletion, but some records may need to be retained where law or legitimate business needs require it.

Depending on your location, you may request access, correction, deletion, withdrawal of consent, restriction, portability or objection to certain processing. We will respond according to applicable law.

Clients outside India may communicate through international platforms. Your information may be processed in India or by third-party providers in other countries, subject to their security and privacy practices.

We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards for client information, but no internet or messaging method is completely risk-free. Do not send sensitive financial passwords, OTPs or unnecessary personal documents through chat.
