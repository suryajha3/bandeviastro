International gemstone consultation FAQ

Questions clients ask before choosing the right page

Can a client buy a gemstone without kundli review?

A client can enquire, but astrological wearing guidance is best discussed after suitability review using birth details and the concern.

Do country gemstone pages replace the shop page?

No. Country pages help clients start from Dubai, USA, UK or Canada searches, while the shop page explains product, quote and order support.

Can certificate preference be discussed before order?

Yes. Certificate preference, stone type, size, metal and setting can be discussed before quote and Booking ID confirmation.

Can I buy directly without kundli?

You can enquire, but wearing for astrological purpose should be done only after suitability review.

Why no fixed price?

Gemstone pricing depends on carat, origin, cut, clarity, treatment and certificate. A quote is safer than fake fixed pricing.

How payment will happen?

Payment is requested only after quote approval. The online payment gateway will be added later; until then staff should share approved payment instructions after confirmation.

Can I order a ring with exact size and metal?

Yes. Share ring size, finger, metal preference, budget and delivery country. Staff should confirm final stone, metal and making details before payment.

Will certificate details be shared before payment?

Certificate preference, carat/ratti, treatment note, origin where available and stone photo or video should be confirmed before quote approval.

Are Neelam and Cat's Eye safe to wear directly?

They are high-caution stones for astrological use. Suitability, trial guidance and wearing method should be discussed before purchase or wearing.

What policies apply to gemstone orders?

Terms, cancellation, refund and shipping policies apply to every paid gemstone order. Review them before payment.
